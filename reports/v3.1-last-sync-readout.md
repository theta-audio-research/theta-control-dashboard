# THETA Control Room — v3.1 Last Sync Readout

## Change

Added a Last Control Room Sync readout to the Today window.

## Source

The readout is driven by:

```text
data/projects.json → lastControlRoomSync
```

## Worker behavior

- `/api/delta` updates `lastControlRoomSync` when project deltas are applied.
- Scheduled checkpoints update `lastControlRoomSync` at:
  - 6 AM
  - 9 AM
  - 12 PM
  - 2 PM
  - 4 PM
  - 11 PM

Timezone: America/Los_Angeles.
